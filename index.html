Frequently Asked Questions

What are the signs of drug abuse?

Common signs include changes in behavior, withdrawal from family and friends, sudden mood swings, and neglect of responsibilities.

How can I help a friend struggling with addiction?

Offer support, listen without judgment, and encourage them to seek professional help. Share resources and be patient.

Where can I find confidential help?

Contact national helplines, local treatment centers, or use the resources listed above for confidential support.
